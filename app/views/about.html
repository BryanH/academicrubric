Admissions Rating Tool

This scoring tool is used to estimate the number of 'points' a candidate may be awarded, based upon various academic criteria. The maximum number of points a candidate may receive is 47.

All candidates are ranked based upon their point score, from greatest to least. Any tie scores are broken the cumulative GPA.

The top candidates, based upon program size and resources will be admitted to the program.

Although academic criteria are vital, candidates are also expected to meet the following requirements, lack of which may disqualify a candidate regardless of point score:

  • Cleared Background Investigation
  • Passed Drug Screening
  • Proof of Health Insurance
  • Complete Immunization Record
  • Fingerprint Review
  • CPR Certification
  • Physical Examination
  • Student Services Orientation Attendance
  • Program Orientation Attendance
