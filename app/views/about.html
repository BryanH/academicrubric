Admissions Rating Tool

This tool is used to determine the number of 'points' a candidate has, based upon various academic criteria.

All candidates are ranked based upon their point score, from greatest to least.

The top candidates, limited by program class size are admitted to the program.

Candidates are still expected to pass the following critera, lack of which may disqualify a candidate regardless of point score:

  • Background investigation
  • Drug Test results
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Overall GPA > x.x
